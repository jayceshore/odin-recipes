Honey-Garlic Slow Cooked Chicken Thighs

Recipe

This dinner dish takes around six hours to cook and ten minutes to prepare. The ingredients yield four servings.

This dish works perfectly for tossing in the slow cooker while you're at work!

Ingredients

To make this delicious dish, you'll need:

  • Four skinless, boneless chick thighs
  • Half a cup of soy sauce
  • Half a cup of ketchup
  • One third of a cup of honey
  • Three cloves of garlic, minced
  • And one teaspoon of dried basil.

Steps

  1. First, lay the chicken thighs into the bottom of an at least four quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk the soy sauce, ketchup, garlic, and basil together and then pour it over the chicken.
  3. Cook on low for six hours.
