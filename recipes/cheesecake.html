New York Cheesecake

Recipe

The recipe for this fabulous cheesecake has a 40 minute prep time, 55 minute cook time, and uses an additional six hours for the dish to be fully ready!

This is not a dish you can make within an hour! This recipe serves 12!

Ingredients

  • One and a half cups of graham cracker crumbs for the crust.
  • One and three fourths cup(s) of white sugar.
  • One third cup of melted butter.
  • Three eight-ounce packages of cream cheese at room temperature.
  • Four eggs, also room temperature.
  • Two tablespoons vanilla extract.
  • And one pint of sour cream.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit or 175 degrees celsius.
  2. Mix together the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and butter in a bowl until everything moistens. Then press into the bottom and a half inch up the sides of a nine-inch pan.
  3. mix together the cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until it's smooth. Then blend in the eggs, tablespoon of vanilla, and cream of tartar. Pour it over the pie crust from step 2.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the center is set, about 50 minutes. Allow it to cool on the counter for around five minutes.
  5. Mix together the sour cream, vanilla extract, and sugar in a bowl until smooth
