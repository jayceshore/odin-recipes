Quick and Easy Grilled Cheese

Recipe

This recipe has a total time of around 16 minutes! Preparation is around ten minutes and then it's necessary to cook for around six minutes.

This is a quick and delicious dish you can make in under 20 minutes and makes a perfect single serving lunch!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread, or more if you would like to make more servings.
  • Two slices of sharp cheddar cheese according to the recipe, or you can swap it out for your favorite type.
  • One tablespoon of each:
    • chopped parsley
    • chopped basil
    • chopped fresh dill
    • oregano
    • rosemary
    • softened butter

Steps

There's only a few steps to this fantastic dish!

  1. Spread half a tablespoon of butter on one side of each piece of bread. Lay the cheese slices on one of the slices on the unbuttered side.
  2. Sprinkle the parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, and fill on the unbuttered side of the other slice of bread.
  3. Then, sandwich the two slices of bread together with the buttered sides outward!
  4. Grab a skillet and heat it over medium. When the skillet gets hot, toss in the sandwich. Cook on each side for around three minutes or until the cheese has melted.
